Step by step guide to doing private sale

If you’re wondering how to sell your home in a private deal in Canada, you’ve got plenty of work ahead of you. But you are going to do this work anyways whehter you do the sale on your own or using a real estate agent. As the homeowner, you play a vital role in preparing your home for the market, determining your asking price, striking the deal, and handing over the role of ownership.

On the other hand, buying a property on a private sale is a relatively easy process. 

Step 1- Prepare

Seller:

  • Use fresh paint to brighten up your home. It will cost you few hundreds but buyers will value it more than that.
  • Clean all the dirty spots to make your space look tidier.
  • Make your home spacious by moving extra stuff to storage.
  • Decorate your home by using paintings, pillows, rugs and plants under small budget.
  • Take beautiful photos of home for listing.

Buyer:

  • It is good to get pre-approved mortgage for stress free process.This also give you added advantage as you can remove the financing condition from the offer and this make you stand out from the pool of buyers.

Step 2 – Gather all documents listed below before listing your home

  • Sales contract
  • Maintenance and repair records
  • 6 month past utility bills
  • Property tax records
  • Mortgage statements
  • Manual and warranties
  • renovation contracts

Step 3 – Estimate sale price of your home

  • Check similar home listings in your neighborhood on real estate website –redfin, Zillow and relator etc. to estimate your home value.
  • Print listing with sold prices of similar sold homes in your neighborhood to represent it to buyer.

Step 4 – Handle Offers

  • Buyer needs real estate lawyer to prepare offer to present it to buyer.
  • Seller needs real estate lawyer to carefully review offer with lawyer to check following information.
    1. Price
    2. Deposit
    3. Down payment
    4. Terms/conditions
    5. Inclusions /exclusions
    6. Closing date
    7. Date of acceptance of offer
  • Seller can make any changes to offer by lawyer then send back it to buyer to accept this offer in specified time.

Step 5 - From offer to closing time

  • Buyer can call home inspector professional to inspect home for issues.

Step 6 - Closing Time

  • Buyer's lawyer is responsible for following tasks:
    1. Ensure the amount is given to trust of seller’s lawyer during closing time.
    2. Prepare deed
    3. Make sure the deal is legal by doing paperwork based on law rules.
    4. Deal with seller lawyer
